# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Ubuntu VPS operators, WordPress developers, and agencies evaluating WPFY before installing it on a disposable server. They arrive skeptical, command-literate, and allergic to hosting-panel marketing.

## Product Purpose

The WPFY marketing website explains the Docker-first WordPress infrastructure CLI, proves operational depth without becoming documentation, states release boundaries honestly, and converts visitors toward the installation guide, GitHub, and forum.

## Brand Personality

Sealed, stamped, operational. Feels like a rackmount faceplate and a field checklist — not a SaaS brochure.

## Anti-references

Cohere-style mineral SaaS, Linear/Vercel/Railway clones, purple-blue gradients, glassmorphism, neon cyberpunk, cartoon doodles, fake dashboards, fake metrics, fake testimonials, decorative terminals with meaningless output, generic feature-card grids.

## Design Principles

- One signature visual: the sealed per-site stack.
- Every major visual must explain isolation, routing, backup, or operations.
- Commands are content, not decoration.
- Separate implemented behavior from deferred or limited behavior.
- Prefer HTML/CSS/SVG technical graphics over raster spectacle.
- Convert toward install docs, GitHub, and forum — not fake lead capture.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.2 AA practical target: visible focus, ≥4.5:1 body contrast, keyboard navigation, reduced-motion alternatives, readable code overflow, clear link labels without decoration dependence.
